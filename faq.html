FAQ (frequently asked questions)

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First, you need to go to this page, log into your account, and after that, you can upload all the files you want to print. After choosing the file, you can set up a couple of more things and confirm your request. Next, you go to the printer (for example on the first floor next to the staircase), scan your card, choose the printing option, and your documents should be printed.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event on Friday 16/09) on the HZ webshop?

Go to the Webshop and there you have to log in to your HZ account. After that, you can purchase whatever you want, and you can pay via PayPal.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID-19?

All necessary information about COVID-19 and related instructions can be found here.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

You can book it from the Portal. When you enter, you can go to 'Make a reservation', after that 'Reserve meeting room or Project Group room' and 'Reserve a room', and then you can choose a room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You need to reveal your HZ pass at the barriers. After that, you can park in the lot.
